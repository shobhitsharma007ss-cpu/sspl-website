What we do

Every stage, under one roof.

A vertically integrated service — yarn to finished, packed garment — with the same standard applied at each step.

01

Knitting

In-house circular knitting across interlock, jersey, pique, rib and fleece, at custom GSM and width — consistent, repeatable quality.

02

Dyeing and Finishing

Eco-conscious dyeing with high colourfastness, bleaching and finishing — zero-liquid-discharge processes calibrated for export consistency.

03

Garment Manufacturing

Cutting, stitching, printing, embroidery and finishing of knitted garments for men, women and kids.

04

Job Work and White-Labelling

For buyers without infrastructure for specific processes — we slot into your supply chain at the same standard.

05

Private Label and OEM/ODM

From your tech pack (OEM) or developed with our in-house development (ODM), with custom labelling and packaging in your identity.

06

Quality Control

A 6-stage QC process — pre-production, in-line and final inspection — before anything leaves the floor.

How it works

From idea to delivery.

STEP 01

Share your idea

Designs, tech pack or a reference sample, plus fabric and finishing details. We revert with a quote.

STEP 02

Sampling and approval

Prototype and pre-production samples to check fit, stitch and quality before bulk begins.

STEP 03

Production and dispatch

Bulk production, staged QC, packed to spec, delivered door-to-door by sea or air.
