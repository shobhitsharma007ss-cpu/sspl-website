Heritage

Trusted since
1989.

Shatabdi Synthetics Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a singular vision — to create a fully integrated textile operation that delivers consistent quality from raw yarn to finished garment. Based in Kanpur, the heartland of India's textile industry, we have grown over 35 years into a name trusted by export houses, national retailers, and global brands.

Our facility handles everything in-house: cotton yarn knitting, dyeing, printing, embroidery, cutting, stitching, and finishing. This vertical integration is what sets us apart — it means fewer middlemen, tighter quality control, faster turnaround, and better pricing for our clients.

"Innovation meets tradition at every step" is not just a tagline — it's how we operate every day.
