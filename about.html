✓ Our Heritage

Trusted

Shatabdi Synthetics Pvt. Ltd. was founded with one vision — a fully integrated textile operation delivering consistent quality from raw yarn to finished garment. Based in Kanpur, the heartland of India's textile industry, we've grown over 35 years into a name trusted by export houses, national retailers and global brands.

Our facility handles everything in-house: knitting, dyeing, printing, embroidery, cutting, stitching and finishing. Fewer middlemen, tighter quality control, faster turnaround, better pricing.
