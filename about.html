Our Story

Built over three decades, under one roof.

From a single integrated facility in Kanpur to a globally certified manufacturer trusted by buyers across 20+ countries.

Since 1989

A single integrated facility, run with intent.

We are Shatabdi Synthetics — a knitted fabric and garment manufacturer based in Kanpur, India. Since 1989 we have operated one integrated facility where yarn becomes fabric, and fabric becomes garment. Knitting, dyeing, bleaching, finishing — every process managed in-house, by specialists dedicated to their craft.

We work with wholesalers, distributors, buying houses and independent buyers. For those without infrastructure for specific fabric processes, we offer job work and white-labelling — with the same standards applied across every order.

Globally certified. Transparently run. Built for buyers who need a manufacturing partner they never have to second-guess.
