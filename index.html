Who we are

We are Shatabdi Synthetics — a manufacturer you never have to second-guess.

Since 1989 we have operated a single integrated facility in Kanpur where knitting, dyeing, bleaching and finishing are all managed in-house, by specialists dedicated to their craft.

We work with wholesalers, distributors, buying houses and independent buyers — and for those without infrastructure for specific processes, we offer job work and white-labelling, held to the same standard across every order.

What we do

Yarn to finished garment, under one roof.

01

Knitting

In-house circular knitting across a wide range of constructions — interlock, jersey, pique, rib and fleece, at custom GSM.

02

Dyeing & Finishing

Eco-conscious dyeing with high colourfastness, bleaching and finishing — calibrated for export-grade consistency.

03

Garment Manufacturing

Cutting, stitching and finishing of knitted garments — t-shirts, leggings, loungewear and workwear for men, women and kids.

04

Job Work & White-Labelling

How it works

A simple, transparent path from idea to delivery.

STEP 01

Share your idea

Send your designs, tech pack or reference samples along with fabric, measurements and finishing details. We revert with a quote.

STEP 02

Sampling & approval

We develop a prototype and pre-production samples for you to check fit, stitch and quality before bulk begins.

STEP 03

Production & dispatch

Bulk production with staged quality checks, packed to your specification and delivered door-to-door by sea or air.
