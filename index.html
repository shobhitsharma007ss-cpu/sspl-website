Knitted Garment and Fabric Manufacturer in India

A vertically integrated clothing manufacturer, trusted since 1989.

Shatabdi Synthetics Pvt. Ltd. (SSPL India) is a knitted fabric and garment manufacturer based in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. For more than three decades we have run a single, fully integrated facility where yarn becomes fabric and fabric becomes finished garment — with knitting, dyeing, bleaching, finishing and stitching all managed in-house. That vertical integration is what lets us offer dependable quality, shorter lead times and competitive pricing to brands, retailers, buying houses and exporters around the world.

OEM and private label clothing manufacturer. As an experienced OEM and private label clothing manufacturer in India, we produce to your tech pack or develop styles with you from scratch (ODM). From fabric selection and sampling to custom labelling, branded packaging and bulk production, we handle the full journey so you can focus on building your brand.

Low MOQ manufacturing for every scale. We support both emerging labels and established buyers. Minimum order quantities start at 500 pieces for startups through our Launchpad Program and 1,000 pieces for standard orders — making us a practical low-MOQ clothing manufacturer for new brands, while scaling comfortably to large wholesale and export volumes.

Knitted garments and cotton fabric. Our core expertise is knitwear. We manufacture t-shirts, polos, leggings, loungewear, activewear, kidswear and workwear, alongside knitted cotton fabric — interlock, jersey, pique, rib and fleece — in custom GSM, widths and colours. Whether you need finished garments or fabric by the roll, every order is held to export-grade standards.

Certified, sustainable and export-ready. We are a globally certified, sustainable garment manufacturer working to GOTS, OEKO-TEX Standard 100, GRS, Textile Exchange and Intertek standards, with SA8000-aligned ethical practices and zero-liquid-discharge dyeing. A six-stage quality-control process means every piece is checked before it leaves the floor, ready for EU and USA markets.

Fast, transparent production. Standard lead times run 45–60 days FOB, with expedited timelines available for larger orders. Share a design, tech pack or reference sample and we revert with a quote, develop pre-production samples for your approval, then move to bulk — with clear communication at every step.

Garment manufacturer in Kanpur, exporting worldwide. From our base in Kanpur — the heart of India's textile industry — we supply customers across the USA, UK, Germany, Australia, the UAE and 15+ other countries. If you're searching for a reliable knitted garment manufacturer, OEM clothing supplier or private label knitwear partner in India, SSPL is built to be a manufacturer you never have to second-guess.
